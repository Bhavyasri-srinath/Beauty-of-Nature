Our Recent Workshop



Connecting to Nature, Connecting to Self

Saturday, May 12, 2018
10.00 AM to 4:00 PM
Camping by the Tea Gardens Ooty,Pykara Lake,Doddabetta Peak
This workshop is set in the beautiful grounds of the Ooty,Tamil nadu.

We will cultivate a deeper sense of connection to our inner landscape
and also the natural world around us.


The workshop will consist of a series of guided practices (indoor and
outdoor), directed tasks, sharing and free exploration. We will explore
as a group how mindful connection with the natural world can be a source
of well-being and nourishment that we can integrate into our lives.


The guided practices are simple and will be suitable for experienced
mindfulness practitioners as well as those who have no prior experience.


The workshop is facilitated by two mindfulness teachers Barry and Evan.

place on the workshop.Cost: 6503 Rs
